Syntax mesothelioma Analysis

The syntax analysis included 6102 abstracts from 6935 published articles. Last update in 2020-06-01 by PubMed. Most of them were Journal Article (70.42%), Review (9.73%), Journal Article | Review (4.85%), Clinical Trial (1.98%) and Multicenter Study (1.92%).

(2) Categorization based on word atomization
(3) Word atomization of each category
(4) Sentece atomization and Human Literature Curation

Categorization Process: The 5 classes of Science Interest

Based on global words tokenization/atomization from abstracts, we categorized the mesothelioma studies in five categories: (1) clinical & signs & symptoms, (2) epidemiology, (3) treatment and (4) diagnosis (Fluxogram 1). The categorization process used the Mesh and DeCS terms list.

Sentece Tokenization

Finally, we peformed the tokenization sentece process from abstracts of each categories.
Frist, we colect the last 4 sentence of each abstract, assumed as the conclusion of the work, using pubmed.mineR. Around 6102 conclusion sentences were achivied.
Second, we extract the sentece context of each category term, previously used, by tokenizer. 2367, 2625, 112, 31 and 1933 senteces were retrivied, about diagnosis, treatment, epidemiology, transmission and clinical, sings and symptoms, respectivelly. Articles with no context sentence were excluded.
